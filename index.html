Our "Must Try" Dishes

Paneer tikka

Most Loved dish from northern-India with smoky flavour

Paneer-sizzler

This recipe has grilled paneer cubes along with stir-fried vegetables and a spicy sauce that is rich in the chili-garlic flavor and served with fried baby mushroom and french fries.

Chicken Biryani

Chicken Biryani is a delicious savory rice dish that is loaded with spicy marinated chicken, caramelized onions, and flavorful saffron rice.

Butter Chicken

Butter chicken or murgh makhani (pronounced [mʊrg ˈmək. kʰə.ni]) is a curry of chicken in a spiced tomato, butter and cream sauce.

Giant Non-Veg Thali

A thali is usually accompanied by small round bowls called katoris, though there are also thalis made with built-in compartments for different dishes, much like a bento box. Our restaurant serves on of the biggest thali in India
